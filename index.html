Hanna

WORLD CLASS YOUNG ARTIST

Born in Minsk, Hanna began studying violin at the Akhremchik Gymnasium of Arts with Galina Goncharova and later moved to Saint-Petersburg where she graduated from the Special School of Music, studio of Prof. Savely Shalman, and the St. Petersburg Rimsky-Korsakov Conservatory, studio of Ilya Ioff. Hanna also completed her bachelor's degree as a Woodruff Scholar at the Schwob School of Music where she studied with Professor Sergiu Schwartz. After a year in Los Angeles, studying at UCLA with Professor Movses Pogossian and Varty Manouelian, and playing with the VEM Ensemble-in-Residence, Hanna moved to Houston, TX where she is currently pursuing her Graduate Performance Certificate in the studio of Dr. Kirsten Yon.
